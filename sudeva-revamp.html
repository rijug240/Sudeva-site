Our Philosophy

BELIEVE.
TRAIN.
RISE.

Founded in 2014 by Anuj Gupta and Vijay Hakari, Sudeva was built to give India's most talented footballers the infrastructure, mentorship, and international opportunities they deserve — regardless of where they come from or what they can afford.



01
Residential Excellence

India's first full-residential football school in Delhi-NCR. A 24/7 environment for physical, technical, and personal growth.

02
Pan-India Scouting

60–75 days per year on the ground across tribal belts, hill states, coastal cities, and metros. Financial background is never a barrier.

03
International Exposure

Bundesliga partnership, Spanish club ownership, Premier League Next Gen Cup, and international training camps.

04
Education & Character

Football is the vehicle. Human development is the mission. Every Sudevan graduates a stronger individual.
